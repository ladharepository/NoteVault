Install on iPhone / iPad
1
Tap the Share button at the bottom of your Safari browser
2
Scroll down in the share sheet and tap "Add to Home Screen"
3
Tap Add in the top right — NoteVault will appear on your home screen like a native app

💡 Note: This only works in Safari on iOS. Chrome on iPhone does not support PWA installation yet.
